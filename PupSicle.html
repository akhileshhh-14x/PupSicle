How PupSicle started and the storyline behind PupSicle

PupSicle is a place where people can see and interact with dogs while they enjoy the food and drinks. It also provide an adoption system for customers who can be potential owners. PupSicle is like a sanctuary providing good experience for customers to make connections with abandoned dogs which will probably lead to adoption.

HOW DID WE COME UP WITH THIS IDEA?
As we have seen in the lockdown many people have abandoned their dogs and that is how we came up with this idea where people can come and adopt dogs as well as enjoy with the ones they already have . Also, people travelling cannot take their dogs everywhere and putting them on the leash isn't fair. So, we as dog lovers would love to see people coming in with their dogs and having a great time with good food and hopefully add another member to their family.
